SOME OF MY RECENT WORK

Here’s how I’ve turned questions into discoveries.

Case Files of Curiosity.

Entertainment Sales Overview

(Quarterly Performance Analysis)

Tools: Excel
This dashboard provides a quarterly overview of ticket sales and revenue for an entertainment service provider. It analyzes performance by movie title, ticket category, location, and time of day, revealing patterns in audience engagement and regional demand. The dashboard improved reporting efficiency and helped identify high‑performing segments for future planning.

Healthcare Financial Overview Dashboard

(Hospital Cost and Profitability Analysis)

Tools: Power BI
This dashboard provides a comprehensive financial overview for a healthcare service provider. Built in Power BI, it visualizes total cost, profit, billings, reimbursement rate, and outstanding claims across multiple hospitals and departments. It highlights profitability by location, monthly sales trends, and cost breakdowns by year and department. The dashboard improved financial transparency, supported better cost management, and enabled leadership to identify high‑performing hospitals and optimize resource allocation.

E‑Commerce Business Overview

(Website Traffic and Conversion Analysis)

Tools: Excel Power Tools
This dashboard analyzes website traffic, user demographics, and conversion performance for an e‑commerce business. It consolidates key metrics such as sessions, page views, conversions, and traffic sources to help identify high‑performing channels and audience segments. The insights support better marketing decisions and improve understanding of user behavior across devices and regions.

Healthcare Revenue & Customer Insights

(Five‑Year Performance Overview)

Tools: Power BI
This dashboard presents a five‑year analysis of revenue and customer behavior for a healthcare service provider. It visualizes performance across gender, geography, customer status, and product categories, offering insights into market distribution and buyer trends. The dashboard supported strategic decision‑making and improved understanding of customer demographics and revenue drivers.

Retail Sales & Marketing Analysis

(Campaign and Category Performance Overview)

Tools: Tableau
This dashboard analyzes retail performance across revenue, units sold, and marketing spend. Built in Tableau, it visualizes monthly trends, campaign effectiveness, and category contributions, helping teams understand how marketing efforts translate into sales outcomes. Key insights include the strong impact of holiday campaigns and the leading performance of jewelry and beauty categories. The dashboard improved marketing budget allocation and provided clearer visibility into seasonal sales patterns and promotional ROI.

Construction Site Performance Dashboard

(Operational Efficiency & Risk Monitoring)

Tools: Power BI, SQL
This dashboard provides a detailed view of site‑level performance, including consumption variability, delivery performance, and operational efficiency. It highlights bottlenecks, identifies risk areas, and supports more accurate scheduling and forecasting decisions. The dashboard enhanced operational oversight and enabled proactive management of site activities.
